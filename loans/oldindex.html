MedNeed Equipment Support Loan

For medical facilities and professionals who need loan for procurement of medical equipment and supplies.

Description

Amount: $1,000 to $40,000 and its equivalent

Interest rate: 6% per annum

Moratorium: 3 months or less

Tenor: 12 months or less

Provided directly by MedNeed for procurement of medical equipment and supplies only.

Available to licensed and certified medical facilities and professionals only.

The health facility finds the equipment or MedNeed helps find the equipment through vendor partners who are pre-accredited for quality assurance.

The health facility may be required to pay part of the cost of the equipment and MedNeed provides the loan to pay the remaining part of the cost.

MedNeed may pay for the full cost of the equipment depending on the amount and the nature of equipment.

The health facility pays MedNeed back in 4 equal installments or less and at stipulated equal intervals within 12 months.

Not paying the required amount at the required time attracts additional 1% penalty for that interval.

Users can seek a new loan only after fully completing payment on an existing loan.

Users who need additional loans can go for the MedNeed Extended Facility Loan or be directed to relevant banks and financing partners.

MedNeed Extended Facility Loan

For medical facilities in need of loan to procure major equipment and for facility development.

Description

Amount: $41,000 to $120,000 and its equivalent

Interest rate: 15% per annum

Moratorium: 6 months or less

Tenor: 30 months or less

Provided directly by MedNeed for procurement of medical equipment and facility development only.

Available to licensed and certified medical facilities and professionals only.

The health facility finds the equipment or MedNeed helps find the equipment through vendor partners who are pre-accredited for quality assurance.

The health facility may be required to pay part of the cost of the equipment and MedNeed provides the loan to pay the remaining part of the cost.

MedNeed may pay for the full cost of the equipment depending on the amount and the nature of equipment.

The health facility pays MedNeed back in 5 equal installments or less and at stipulated equal intervals within 30 months.

Not paying the required amount at the required time attracts additional 1% penalty for that interval.

Users can seek new a loan only after fully completing payment on an existing loan.

Users who need additional loans can be directed to relevant banks and financing partners.
