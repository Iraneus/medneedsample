Loans

Medical facilities can choose from two main types of loans; Instant and Variable.


Instant Loan

Main features

Can be accessed immediately or as soon as same day of request.

Available to pre-approved medical facilities.

Set interest rate at 4% percent.

Set moratorium (time before repayment starts) of 1 month maximum.

Set tenor (duration of loan) of 4 months i.e. loan must be fully paid back within 4 months.

Available for equipment and supplies procured from pre-approved vendors and manufacturers.

Available in selected locations.

Please contact MedNeed support to verify.

Learn more


Variable Loan

Main features

Terms vary depending on the lenders/investors.

Can be accessed within a week of request.

Available to all medical facilities.

Interest rate varies.

Moratorium (time before repayment starts) varies.

Set tenor (duration of loan) varies.

Available for equipment and supplies procured from any vendor and manufacturer.
