Getting Loans: How it works

Medical facilities can quickly receive loans for medical equipment from lenders/investors on MedNeed.

Basic Steps

The medical facility completes and submits application.

MedNeed confirms the medical facility and the equipment need.

MedNeed works with the medical facility to find the most suitable vendor or manufacturer for the equipment.

MedNeed indentifies lenders/investors to fulfill the loan request.

Interested lenders/investors review and fulfill the loan request.

The medical facility accepts the most suitable loan which could be provided by an individual lender/investor or a group of lenders/investors.

The funds are made available for the equipment as soon as the same day or within a week of the loan acceptance.

MedNeed works with the medical facility to ensure that the equipment is procured and delivered accordingly.

The medical facility starts repayments as agreed in the terms of loan.


Key Benefits

Support and guidance throughout the process, from getting loans to acquiring needed medical equipment.

Faster access to loan with minimal stress for the medical facility.

Negotiation of better terms for loans including lower interest rate and increased moratorium.

Negotiation of better prices and quality assurance on equipment from certified vendors/manufacturers.

The medical equipment can be used as collateral based on the nature of the loan and the requirements from the lenders/investors.

MedNeed works with the medical facility to ensure that the equipment is bought from the right vendor or manufacturer at a reasonable price and that the equipment is covered by reasonable warranty especially if the equipment is used as collateral depending on the lender/investor requirements.


Paying MedNeed

The medical facility pays MedNeed a flat fee of $49 (and its equivalent) only.

Payment is made after a suitable loan is accepted.


Basic Prequalification

The medical facility must be dully registered and meet all applicable regulatory and professional criteria.

The director/representative of the medical facility must be a certified member of relevant and accredited medical bodies.

The medical facility must show due need for the equipment since funds are only for medical equipment.

The medical facility must meet any specific requirements by lenders/investors when necessary.

The medical facility must be agree to abide by the terms of the loan and make repayment as and when due.

The medical facility must understand that MedNeed will work with lenders/investors to collect payments from borrowers who are delinquent or in default using every applicable legal and professional avenue possible.
