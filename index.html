About MedNeed

MedNeed is improving access to medical care in communities by providing financing solutions including offers, loans and other cost-saving programs to enable more health facilities and professionals acquire the equipment and supplies they need to deliver improved and affordable medical care to more people.

MedNeed is a great resource for users to turn to for information on a wide range of financial support and assistance programs for medical equipment and supplies.
