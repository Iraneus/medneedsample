About MedNeed

MedNeed is a peer-to-peer lending platform with lenders, investors, vendors and development partners providing financing solutions to enable medical facilities to easily acquire the equipment and supplies they need.

MedNeed was founded with the aim providing solutions that make it easier for medical facilities to have what they need to deliver improved and affordable medical care to more people.
