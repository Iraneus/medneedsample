About MedNeed

MedNeed is a financing and payments platform for medical equipment and supplies.

MedNeed was founded with the aim of providing solutions that make it easier for medical facilities to have what they need to deliver improved medical care to more people.

Medical facilities can benefit from financing and equipment incentives from MedNeed, lenders, investors, vendors and development partners.

MedNeed makes financing more accessible and inclusive and empowers medical facilities to acquire the things they need at the time they need them.
