Credit Payment

Medical facilities can receive immediate credit for medical equipment and supplies procurement.


Main features

Can be accessed immediately or as soon as same day of request.

Available to pre-approved medical facilities.

Maximum amount is $1000.

Set interest rate of 4% percent maximum.

Set moratorium (time before repayment starts) of 1 month maximum.

Set tenor (duration of loan) of 4 months i.e. loan must be fully paid back within 4 months.

Repayment is made in maximum of 4 equal parts or less.

Available for equipment and supplies procured from pre-approved vendors.

Available in selected locations.

Please contact MedNeed support to verify.


Basic Steps

The medical facility completes and submits application.

MedNeed confirms the medical facility and the equipment need.

MedNeed works with the medical facility to select the most suitable vendor for the equipment.

The funds are made available for the equipment instantly or as soon as same day of request.

MedNeed works with the medical facility to ensure that the equipment is procured and delivered accordingly.

The medical facility starts repayments as agreed in the terms of loan.


Key Benefits

Support and guidance throughout the process, from getting the credit to acquiring the medical equipment.

Faster access to credit for immediate equipment and supplies procurement.

Better terms including lower interest rate and reasonable moratorium.

Better prices and quality assurance on equipment from certified vendors.

MedNeed works with the medical facility to ensure that the equipment is bought from the right vendor at a reasonable price and that the equipment is covered by reasonable warranty.


Basic Prequalification

Available only to medical facilities in Ghana, Kenya, Nigeria and South Africa.

The medical facility must be duly registered and meet all applicable local regulatory and professional requirements.

The director/representative of the medical facility must be a certified member of an accredited medical association.

The medical facility must show due need for the equipment since funds are only for medical equipment and supplies.

The medical facility must agree to abide by the terms and make repayment as and when due.

The medical facility must understand that MedNeed will collect payments from borrowers who are delinquent or in default using every applicable regulatory and legal avenue possible.
