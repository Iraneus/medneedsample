Borrow Equipment

MedNeed helps medical facilities to find and borrow equipment from other owners for temporary use.

Basic Steps

The medical facility places request.

MedNeed identifies owners willing to lend their equipment when available.

MedNeed sends the terms from the lenders to the borrower.

The borrower accepts the terms from the most suitable lender.

The borrower makes payment in accordance with the terms.

MedNeed works with the borrower to pick up the equipment.

The borrower returns the equipment when due in accordance with the terms.


Please contact MedNeed Support if you need to borrow equipment or have equipment to lend to other users.
