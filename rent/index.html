Rent Equipment

MedNeed helps medical facilities to find and rent equipment from other owners for temporary use.


Basic Steps

The medical facility places request.

MedNeed sends request to owners available to lend their equipment.

MedNeed sends the terms from the lenders to the renter.

The renter accepts the terms from the most suitable lender.

The renter makes payment in accordance with the terms.
