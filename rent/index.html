Rent Equipment

Medical facilities can rent equipment from owners for temporary use.


Main features

Renters can request to rent on credit or pay immediately.

Credit is available only to pre-approved medical facilities.


Basic Steps

The medical facility places request.

MedNeed sends request to owners available to lend their equipment.

MedNeed sends terms from lenders to the renter.

The renter accepts the terms from the most suitable lender.

The renter makes payment in accordance with the terms.
